Body

Design

We use Fusion 360 to design our robots.

In this app, you can create 3D model of what you want to make.

Then, you can also export the file in other file types, f3d, stl, dxf, etc.

Frame

We make frames from boards of alminium using CNC machine.

This frame can stand strong impact during the game.

Motor

We use pololu 25D 20.4 : 1 motors which can defeat other robots in pushing each other.

Micro Computer, Sensor

All of the sensors and microcomputers are the product of DAISEN electronic industry company.

They are easy to use for beginners, but there is longer delay between sensors and micro computer.

We have to make efforts to write shorter program so that robots can process more speedy.

Other parts

Other things, such as covers, the mount to set sensors, or omuni wheels, are made by 3D printer.

Electronic Circuit

Design

We use KiCad to make circuit diagram and pcb.

We just made line sensors and DCDC converters using LED, phototransister, resister, condenser, and so on.

Ordering

After making the pcb on KiCad, we order JLCPCB to make them. About one or two weeks later, the boards will have been delivered, and we do soldering.
